About Me

Rose Monet Little is the founder of ClearPath Systems, where AI-powered workflow automation is designed to make business operations more efficient and stress-free. As a professional in public health and program evaluation, Rose brings a unique perspective that ensures every system is built with both people and performance in mind.

ClearPath Systems was created to help entrepreneurs and organizations reduce repetitive tasks, simplify processes, and focus on what truly matters. By blending technology with a human-centered approach, Rose empowers clients to reclaim their time, improve productivity, and grow with confidence.
